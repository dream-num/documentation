## What is Univer Slides

Univer is a full-stack framework to build productivity applications, and Univer Slides is the presentation component of Univer.

Univer Slides can both run in the browser and on the server (Node.js). It is designed to be highly flexible, extensible, and customizable, allowing you to build a wide range of applications, from simple text entry to complex typesetting.
